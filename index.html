WAR STORIES

War Stories by Gordon Korman is a book about a boy named Trevor, who's great grandpa was a war hero from WW2. Trevor's great grandfather (or "GG") has been welcomed back to France to get a medal for a town called St. Regine he liberated back in 1944. Trevor, who loves WW2 and his GG's stories, urges him to go. Little did they know, a group called "La Vérité" is trying to stop them not to come. And the closer they get to St Regine, the wierder GG gets. Nobody knows, but a decision haunts GG for life...

by Gordon Korman
